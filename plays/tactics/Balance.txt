Metadata:
type tactic
name Balance

Parameters:
bot robot
bot_number double

Objects:
perimeter (circle (ourgoal) 0.7)
ballpoint (pointof ball)
ballline (line ballpoint (ourgoal))
wallmainpt (linecircleintersection ballline perimeter -1)
wallcircle (circle wallmainpt 0.1)
twomanpt (if_point (= bot_number 0.0) (circlecircleintersection wallcircle perimeter -1) (circlecircleintersection wallcircle perimeter 1))
twomanbotpt (pointof bot)

# is defender going in to kick
defensedisttoball (pointpointdistance twomanbotpt ballpoint)

# should robot kick
do_kick  (and (< defensedisttoball 0.2) (= bot_number 0.0))

Actions:
(if_Action do_kick (robotpointkick bot (theirgoal)) (robotpointpointmove bot twomanpt ballpoint))
